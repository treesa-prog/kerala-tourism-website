Alappuzha (Alleppey)

Alappuzha, popularly known as the "Venice of the East", is famous for its serene backwaters, houseboat cruises, and beautiful beaches. It is one of the top tourist destinations in Kerala, offering a peaceful and scenic experience.

Backwaters & Houseboats

Experience the famous houseboat cruises through the calm backwaters surrounded by coconut trees.

Alappuzha Beach

A beautiful beach with a historic pier, lighthouse, and sunset views.

Vembanad Lake

The longest lake in India, perfect for boating and scenic views.
