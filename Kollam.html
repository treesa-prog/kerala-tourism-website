Kollam

Kollam is a historic coastal district of Kerala, known for its backwaters, beaches, and trade heritage. It is home to the famous Ashtamudi Lake and serves as a gateway to the scenic backwater destinations of Kerala.

Ashtamudi Lake

A beautiful backwater destination famous for houseboat cruises and scenic beauty. It is the second largest lake in Kerala.

Jatayu Earth's Center

A famous tourist attraction featuring the world’s largest bird sculpture, based on the legend of Jatayu from the Ramayana.

Thangassery Lighthouse

A historic lighthouse offering panoramic views of the Arabian Sea. It is one of the tallest lighthouses in Kerala.
