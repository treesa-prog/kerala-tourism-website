Thiruvananthapuram

Thiruvananthapuram, the capital city of Kerala, is located in the southwestern tip of India and is known for its beaches, historic monuments, backwaters, and rich cultural heritage.

Kovalam Beach

One of the most famous beaches in Kerala, Kovalam is known for its crescent-shaped coastline, lighthouse views, and peaceful atmosphere. A perfect spot for relaxation and sunsets.

Padmanabhaswamy Temple

A world-famous temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu, known for its stunning Dravidian architecture and rich history. It is one of the richest temples in the world.

Ponmudi Hills

A scenic hill station near Trivandrum, famous for its winding roads, misty mountains, and lush greenery. Ideal for trekking and nature lovers.
