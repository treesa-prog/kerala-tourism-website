Pathanamthitta

Pathanamthitta, known as the Pilgrim Capital of Kerala, is famous for its temples, forests, and scenic landscapes. It is home to Sabarimala, one of the most visited pilgrimage centers in India.

Sabarimala Temple

One of the largest pilgrimage centers in the world dedicated to Lord Ayyappa. Millions of devotees visit every year.

Gavi

A beautiful eco-tourism destination known for forests, wildlife, and peaceful lakes. Ideal for nature lovers and trekking.

Aruvikkuzhy Waterfalls

A scenic waterfall surrounded by lush greenery, perfect for picnics and relaxation.
